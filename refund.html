Refund Policy

This Refund Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for refunds in relation to the use of TrustShield Technology's Saas product available on the website [https://verifiedproof.net](https://verifiedproof.net) (the "Website"). Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

1. Pricing and Payment

1.1. Pay per Use Model: Our Saas product operates on a pay per use model, where you are charged based on the specific services or features you utilize. The pricing details are clearly communicated on our Website or during the checkout process.

1.2. No Automatic Refunds: Due to the nature of our pay per use model, we do not offer automatic refunds for payments made unless required by applicable law.

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1. Account Closure: In the event that your account is closed or terminated by TrustShield Technology, you may be eligible for a refund of any remaining credits in your account balance. However, no refunds will be provided for any amounts already utilized or consumed by you.

3. Refund Process

3.1. Requesting a Refund: If you believe you are eligible for a refund based on the account closure criteria mentioned above, you must contact our support team at support@verifiedproof.net and provide the necessary details to initiate the refund process.

3.2. Refund Determination: The refund eligibility will be determined by TrustShield Technology, considering the circumstances of the account closure and the remaining credits in your account balance. TrustShield Technology reserves the right to approve or deny refund requests at its sole discretion.

3.3. Refund Amount: If your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed for the amount corresponding to the remaining credits in your account balance. Any fees or charges associated with the refund process, such as transaction fees, will be deducted from the refund amount.

3.4. Refund Method: Refunds will generally be issued using the original payment method used for the transaction. However, TrustShield Technology may choose to refund via alternative methods at its discretion.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

4.1. Non-Utilization: If you have not utilized or consumed any credits within your account balance and choose to close your account voluntarily, no refunds will be provided.

4.2. Violation of Terms of Service: If your account closure is a result of a violation of our Terms of Service or any applicable laws, no refunds will be provided.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Refund Policy, please contact our support team at support@verifiedproof.net.

Please note that this Refund Policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage you to review the latest version of this policy periodically.
