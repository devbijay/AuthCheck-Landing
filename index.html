  • What is product authentication, and why do I need it?

    Product authentication is the process of verifying the authenticity of a product, typically through the use of unique codes or labels. It's important because counterfeit products can damage your brand's reputation, lead to legal issues, and even harm customers.

  • How does your platform work?

    Our platform uses a combination of QR codes, mobile technology, and cloud-based authentication to verify the authenticity of products. Customers can simply scan the QR code on the product packaging with their smartphone, and our platform will authenticate the product in real-time.

  • Can your platform integrate with my existing systems?

  • How much does your platform cost?
